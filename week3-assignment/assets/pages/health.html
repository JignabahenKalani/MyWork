Healthcare for the Underserved

Access to basic healthcare should be a right, not a privilege. Smile NGO's health initiatives focus on providing essential medical services, spreading awareness, and preventing disease in vulnerable communities.

Free Medical Camps

We organize regular health camps offering checkups, treatment, and free medicine for people who can't afford basic care.

Health Awareness Drives

We conduct health education sessions on hygiene, nutrition, and preventive care in schools and villages.

Clinic Partnerships

Partnering with local clinics and hospitals allows us to refer patients for long-term care at reduced or no cost.

Be the Reason Someone Receives Care

Help us bring smiles to the sick and needy. Your support ensures that no one is left behind in their health journey.
